Cooking is too complicated. Sometimes I'll be in the middle of making something and want to cry because I no longer want to do it and know it'll turn out tasteless anyway because the mummy blog I got the recipe off called for a teaspoon of salt and no more
So here are the things that are so easy even I can do them.

Method 3 - using display:table and height for animation

01. Chop a shitload of carrots and celery-- usually I do three of each
02. Then you chop an onion
03. Put heaps of olive oil in a heavy-bottomed pot on medium heat and chuck in your veg
04. Stir them for like three minutes. When the onion's going translucent, put in
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp ground fennel
  • Some saffron if you have it
  • I lied about the amounts!
  • Put in what feels right
05. Whatever you put in, now put in more. Once you've mixed it in and it smells really good, put in 2 tbsp tomato paste and a tin of tomatoes including their juice. Stir that 06. Drain a can of chickpeas, put that in. Any dried lentils in the house? Add a handful. Put in 750mL of veg stock 07. Boil, then simmer for 20 mins 08. While that's happening, chop up whatever green herbs you can find. Just go stupid with a big knife. Once the pot's finished, stir through that, minced garlic and minced ginger from jars. You're done. You will never ever taste a better soup
